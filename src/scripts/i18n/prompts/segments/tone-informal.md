# TONE: INFORMAL ADDRESS

Use INFORMAL address (T-V distinction) when addressing the reader. This content has a conversational, approachable tone matching ethereum.org's friendly voice.

CRITICAL: Maintain informal address CONSISTENTLY throughout the entire document. Never mix formal and informal forms. All verb conjugations, possessives, and imperatives addressing the reader must use the informal form.
