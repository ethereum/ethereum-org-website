When mainstream crowdfunding platforms froze a high-profile fundraiser, supporters looked for an alternative that couldn't be switched off. Ethereum offered exactly that.

## Donations that can't be frozen {#donations-that-cant-be-frozen}

Contributions continued to arrive from supporters who wanted their donations to reach their intended recipients, settled on a network that no single party controls.

## Why it matters {#why-it-matters}

Whatever your view of any particular cause, the episode showed something fundamental: open networks let people transact without asking permission.
