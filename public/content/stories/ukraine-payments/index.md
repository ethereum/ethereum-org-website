# Ukraine payments during the war {#ukraine-payments-during-the-war}

> This is a placeholder story used while the full Human Stories layout is being designed. The copy below is illustrative.

In the early days of the war, traditional payment systems were strained and slow. Donations sent through Ethereum arrived in minutes, reaching organizations and individuals directly.

## Funds that move at the speed of need {#funds-that-move-at-the-speed-of-need}

Volunteers coordinated supplies, medical aid, and shelter funded by contributions from around the world — settled transparently, without intermediaries deciding who could send or receive.

## Why it matters {#why-it-matters}

In a crisis, speed and openness save lives. Ethereum gave people a way to help each other when the usual systems couldn't keep up.
