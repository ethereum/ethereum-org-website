Nyaraka hizi zimeundwa ili kukusaidia kujenga na [Ethereum](/). Inashughulikia Ethereum kama dhana, inaelezea tech stack ya Ethereum, na kuweka kumbukumbu za mada za hali ya juu kwa programu tumizi na matumizi changamano zaidi.

Kila kitu hapa ni chanzo-wazi (open-source) na kinadumishwa na jamii, kwa hivyo ikiwa ukurasa umepitwa na wakati au unakosa kitu muhimu, fungua issue au pull request. [Mwongozo wa kuhariri](https://github.com/ethereum/ethereum-org-website/blob/dev/docs/editing-markdown.md) unaelezea jinsi ya kufanya hivyo.

## Chagua mahali pa kuanzia {#pick-a-starting-point}

Wasomaji huja na malengo tofauti, na njia ya haraka zaidi kupitia nyaraka hizi inategemea kile unachotaka kujenga. Baadhi ya sehemu za kawaida za kuanzia:

- **Kujenga programu tumizi iliyogatuliwa (dapp) inayowasiliana na Ethereum.** Anza na [utangulizi wa kiufundi](/developers/docs/intro-to-ethereum/), kisha pitia [akaunti](/developers/docs/accounts/) na [miamala](/developers/docs/transactions/). Chagua [framework](/developers/docs/frameworks/) unapokuwa tayari kuandika kodi.
- **Kuandika mkataba mahiri.** Pitia haraka [utangulizi](/developers/docs/intro-to-ethereum/) ikiwa dhana za EVM ni ngeni kwako, kisha ruka hadi kwenye [mikataba mahiri](/developers/docs/smart-contracts/) na [lugha ya programu](/developers/docs/programming-languages/).
- **Kuendesha nodi au uwekaji dhamana.** Nenda kwenye [nodi na wateja (clients)](/developers/docs/nodes-and-clients/), kisha [mitandao](/developers/docs/networking-layer/) na [mifumo ya mwafaka](/developers/docs/consensus-mechanisms/).
- **Kuelewa itifaki kuanzia chini kwenda juu.** Moduli zilizo hapa chini zimepangwa kwa ajili ya hili. Zisome kwa mfuatano.
