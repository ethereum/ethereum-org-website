Linapokuja suala la kusanidi [mazingira jumuishi ya uendelezaji (IDE)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrated_development_environment), kupanga programu kwenye Ethereum ni sawa na kupanga mradi mwingine wowote wa programu. Kuna chaguzi nyingi za kuchagua, kwa hivyo mwisho wa siku, chagua IDE au kihariri cha msimbo kinachofaa zaidi mapendeleo yako. Kuna uwezekano mkubwa chaguo bora la IDE kwa uendelezaji wako wa Ethereum ni IDE ambayo tayari unatumia kwa uendelezaji wa programu za kitamaduni.

## IDE za kwenye Wavuti {#web-based-ides}

Ikiwa unatafuta kuchezea msimbo kabla ya [kusanidi mazingira ya uendelezaji ya ndani](/developers/local-environment/), programu hizi za wavuti zimeundwa maalum kwa ajili ya uendelezaji wa mkataba mahiri wa Ethereum.

**[Remix](https://remix.ethereum.org/)** - **_IDE ya kwenye wavuti iliyo na uchanganuzi tuli uliojengewa ndani, na mashine pepe ya majaribio ya mnyororo wa vitalu_**

- [Nyaraka](https://remix-ide.readthedocs.io/en/latest/#)
- [Gitter](https://gitter.im/ethereum/remix)

**[ChainIDE](https://chainide.com/)** - **_IDE ya minyororo mingi inayotegemea wingu_**

- [Nyaraka](https://chainide.gitbook.io/chainide-english-1/)
- [Jukwaa la msaada](https://forum.chainide.com/)

**[Replit (Solidity Starter - Beta)](https://replit.com/@replit/Solidity-starter-beta)** - **_Mazingira ya uendelezaji yanayoweza kubinafsishwa kwa ajili ya Ethereum yenye upakiaji upya wa haraka, ukaguzi wa makosa, na usaidizi wa daraja la kwanza wa mtandao wa majaribio_**

- [Nyaraka](https://docs.replit.com/)

**[Tenderly Sandbox](https://sandbox.tenderly.co/)** - **_Mazingira ya haraka ya kuunda mifano ambapo unaweza kuandika, kutekeleza, na kutatua mikataba mahiri kwenye kivinjari ukitumia Solidity na JavaScript_**

**[EthFiddle](https://ethfiddle.com/)** - **_IDE ya kwenye wavuti inayokuruhusu kuandika, kukusanya, na kutatua mkataba mahiri wako_**

- [Gitter](https://gitter.im/loomnetwork/ethfiddle)

## IDE za kwenye Kompyuta {#desktop-ides}

IDE nyingi zilizoimarika zimeunda programu-jalizi ili kuboresha uzoefu wa uendelezaji wa Ethereum. Kwa uchache, hutoa uangaziaji wa sintaksia kwa [lugha za mkataba mahiri](/developers/docs/smart-contracts/languages/).

**Visual Studio Code -** **_IDE ya kitaalamu ya majukwaa mtambuka yenye usaidizi rasmi wa Ethereum_**

- [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/)
- [Sampuli za msimbo](https://github.com/Azure-Samples/blockchain/blob/master/blockchain-workbench/application-and-smart-contract-samples/readme.md)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode)

**IDE za JetBrains (IntelliJ IDEA, n.k.) -** **_Zana muhimu kwa waendelezaji wa programu na timu_**

- [JetBrains](https://www.jetbrains.com/)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/JetBrains)
- [IntelliJ Solidity](https://github.com/intellij-solidity/intellij-solidity/)

**Remix Desktop -** **_Pata uzoefu wa Remix IDE kwenye mashine yako ya ndani_**

- [Pakua](https://github.com/ethereum/remix-desktop/releases)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/ethereum/remix-desktop)

## Programu-jalizi na viendelezi {#plugins-extensions}

- [solidity](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=JuanBlanco.solidity) - Lugha ya Ethereum Solidity kwa ajili ya Visual Studio Code
- [Solidity + Hardhat for VS Code](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=NomicFoundation.hardhat-solidity) - Usaidizi wa Solidity na Hardhat kutoka kwa timu ya Hardhat
- [Prettier Solidity](https://github.com/prettier-solidity/prettier-plugin-solidity) - Kiumbizo cha msimbo kinachotumia prettier
