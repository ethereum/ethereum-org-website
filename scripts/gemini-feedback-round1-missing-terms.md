# Gemini Feedback: Missing Terms (Round 1)

45 additional terms suggested by Gemini that are missing from the extraction.

## accounts-keys
1. **Hardware wallet** - Often translated as "physical wallet" or "cold wallet"
2. **Hot wallet / Cold wallet** - Literally translated as "warm/chilled" loses technical context
3. **Derivation path** - Often mistranslated as a "route" or "way"
4. **Key rotation** - Often translated as "key turning" rather than systematic replacement
5. **Passkey** - Newer standard (WebAuthn); needs distinction from passwords/seed phrases

## consensus
6. **Inactivity leak** - Gradual loss of stake vs one-time penalty
7. **Churn limit** - Validator entry/exit queue; often vaguely translated
8. **Attestation penalty** - Must be distinguished from "slashing"
9. **Liquid restaking** - Adding "liquid" to already-hard "restaking"
10. **Re-org (Chain reorganization)** - "Rearrangement" doesn't capture block deletion

## cryptography
11. **Trusted setup** - Needs consistent term for one-time initialization phase
12. **Commitment (e.g., KZG commitment)** - "Promise" misses mathematical "binding" nature
13. **Precompile** - Must avoid confusion with "pre-compiled code"
14. **Transcript** - ZK context; often mistranslated as "summary"

## defi
15. **Slippage** - One of the most inconsistently translated terms
16. **Price impact** - Distinct from slippage, often conflated
17. **Peg / De-peg** - Highly idiomatic; physical "hook" translations confusing
18. **Overcollateralization** - Needs standardized term
19. **Provisioning liquidity** - Often translated as "giving" or "sending" money

## development
20. **Boilerplate** - Translated literally as "plate of boiling water"
21. **Mainnet-forking** - Translated as "splitting" rather than "simulating"
22. **Sandbox** - Translated as literal "box of sand"
23. **Production-ready** - Critical for security warnings
24. **Localhost** - Needs consistent local-language equivalent

## general/ecosystem
25. **On-ramp / Off-ramp** - Hardest metaphor to translate; literal "ramps"
26. **Onboarding** - Translated as "training" or "hiring" instead of first interaction
27. **Self-sovereignty** - "Independence" doesn't capture ownership of identity/assets
28. **Permissioned** - Needs to contrast with "Permissionless"
29. **Trust-minimized** - "Low trust" sounds negative
30. **State bloat** - "Bloat" translated as "swelling" or "illness"
31. **Statelessness** - Must not imply "no country"

## scaling
32. **Throughput** - Often translated as "performance" (too broad)
33. **Settlement** - L2->L1 finalization; "payment" misses the point
34. **Batching** - "Stacking" or "grouping" misses technical efficiency
35. **Fault proof** - Modern term for "fraud proof"; needs standardization
36. **Escape hatch** - L2 safety mechanism; sounds like physical emergency exit
37. **Forced withdrawal** - Must sound like "protocol right" not "aggressive act"

## smart-contracts
38. **Reentrancy guard** - Mechanism to prevent the attack
39. **Self-destruct** - Old term "suicide" still used; needs standard "delete/remove"
40. **Call data** - "Phone data" or "shouting data" mistranslations
41. **Gas optimization** - "Making gas better" vs reducing costs

## tokens
42. **Vesting** - "Clothing" from Latin root; needs "locked tokens released over time"
43. **Cliff** - Period before vesting; "geographical cliff" translations
44. **Fractionalization** - Breaking NFT into pieces
45. **Metadata** - "Data about data" too wordy for UI
